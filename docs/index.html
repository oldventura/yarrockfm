WHAT THE F*CK IS THIS?

Alright, this is technically not a radio. I just like to create playlists and share them with people. So I thought, why not create a web service that functions like an internet radio without actually streaming anything?

So, here's a trick I've found out: You can create special YouTube links that create playlists on the run without needing an account. So, I just prepare some songs and the rest is done by the website. You can open those playlists, listen to them, add to your library and maybe change them. It's up to you!

Every week I decide on a theme and create a playlist from songs that can be related to that theme. You can check out the archive to find out new playlists and also explore old ones.

This is just a project for fun. There's no hidden catch; I don't collect any information about users and I am not trying to profit from this service. Just listen to some good music and enjoy.
